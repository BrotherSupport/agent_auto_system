How it works

From idea to execution in three steps

01

Define a job

Choose an automation type and supply its inputs — a URL, a keyword, recipients, or a pipeline of steps.

02

Trigger a run

Hit run. The job is queued and an agent crew kicks off in the background via POST /api/jobs/{id}/run.

03

Watch & collect

Progress streams live over SSE. When it finishes, review the result, logs, costs, and quality score.
